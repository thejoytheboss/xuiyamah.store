// FAQ

What is pay what you can? [ + ]

Pay what you can means you set the price. There is no minimum — $0 is accepted. A suggested price is listed for each item to cover materials and time, but you pay whatever feels right. Art should be accessible to everyone.

How do commissions work? [ + ]

Place an order using the "describe it" option and tell me what you want — colors, vibes, symbols, references. You can upload a reference image. Turnaround is 2–3 weeks. For complex pieces, reach out via Instagram @demonmercenary first.

Do you ship worldwide? [ + ]

Yes. Physical prints and handmade decor ship worldwide from Atlanta, GA. Digital downloads are delivered instantly. Shipping is included in the suggested price for physical items.

What is the Surprise Me option? [ + ]

Surprise Me lets you trust the artist completely. Pick mood tags like dark, celestial, chaotic or tender to nudge the direction — or choose nothing for full creative freedom. You receive an original piece made from wherever the artist's head is at.

Where is Xuiyamah based? [ + ]

Xuiyamah is an independent art marketplace run by demonmercenary, based in Atlanta, Georgia. All handmade items are crafted in Atlanta and shipped worldwide.
